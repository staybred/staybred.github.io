About Llewellyn

Born in Bartow, Florida and raised in Newport News, Virginia. Llewellyn C. Radford II broke into the entertainment industry as a model while attending Hampton University. Pushed by his mother to attend an open casting call, Llewellyn gained his first acting experience as a background actor on the $30 Million Warner Bros picture The Box starring Cameron Diaz, James Marsden, and Frank Langella. The experience allowed him to discover his passion for acting.

While on a spontaneous trip to Chicago for the 4th of July in 2008 he cheated on modeling with acting and has never looked back. In October 2009, he stepped out on faith and decided to move to Los Angeles, California when his close friend DJ Tay James was hired as Justin Bieber's official DJ. He landed at LAX with $500 and a single bag. .

The past five years have been an incredible journey for Llewellyn. In that time he has been involved in numerous creative projects from acting, song writing, and spoken word poetry. To this day Llewellyn continues to chase his dreams and lives to inspire others every step of the way.
